# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Gulf employers and sponsors reviewing the employment standing of محمد عبد العظيم إبراهيم وسية, an advertising designer (مصمم دعاية) based in Jubail, Saudi Arabia. They open the page once, scan it in under a minute, and judge both the facts and the person by how the page carries itself. Secondary: the family, who keep and share the record. All readers are Arabic-speaking; the page is Arabic-only, RTL.

## Product Purpose

A single-page personal record (سجل توظيف — نسخة شخصية) that presents the facts of an employment letter dated 22 May 2026: employee details, employer details, and the letter text. It exists so those facts can be shared and printed cleanly instead of forwarding a photo of paper. Success is a reviewer trusting the page within ten seconds and finding any specific field without hunting.

## Positioning

The employment facts, typeset so carefully that the page itself is evidence of professionalism.

## Brand Personality

مُتقَن (meticulous), رسمي (formal), هادئ (calm). The voice is that of a well-kept file: precise, unhurried, never promotional. Confidence comes from restraint and typographic order, not decoration.

## Anti-references

Flashy portfolio effects, SaaS landing-page grammar (gradients, glassmorphism, card grids), and anything that imitates an official issued document — no letterheads, logos, stamps, or signature blocks belonging to the issuing company. The page is always a clearly-labeled personal copy, never a reproduction of the letter itself.

## Design Principles

- Typography carries the professionalism; every other element defers to it.
- Restraint is credibility: one accent color, hairline structure, generous whitespace.
- Print is a first-class output — the page must produce a flawless A4 sheet.
- The "نسخة شخصية" (personal copy) framing stays visible at all times.
- Numbers are data: monospaced, exact, never restyled decoratively.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast throughout (≥4.5:1 for all text including labels). Full RTL correctness, including digit runs inside Arabic text. `prefers-reduced-motion` honored. Semantic landmarks and heading structure for screen readers. Touch targets ≥44px.
